Why do you use Chef?

With Chef's pre-store week-long recipe selection, in-store mapping feature, and out-of-store cooking features, Chef becomes your best friend when it comes to how you eat.

First, you pick your cooking package.

This cooking package gives you enough food and recipes for an extended period of time - let's say a week for this example.

Now that you have picked what kind of food you want to cook for the week, we'll go with you to the store to make your life easier.

First, we condence items and help you bulk buy. This way you save money cooking great recipes for cheaper. Then, we point you to where these items are in the store. We know you're busy, and we want to help you get out of that store. Heck, we're hungry too and tired too. Finally, we'll also recommend some items throughout the store (that are on your way) that you might want to add on to your package.

Phew, so what's next!? We aren't going to ditch you just yet. Now that you've bought your groceries and gotten home, we will help you cook the new recipes.

We want you to become your own master chef. We want your kids, family, or friends to be amazed at just how good of a cook you are becoming. With your package comes videos of master chefs cooking those same recipes that you know how the groceries for. We're going to cook with you and make this fun.

Oh, and did I mention that there's an online avatar joining you throughout this process.

Oh wait! One more thing. You aren't doing this alone. A major percentage of what you spend through this app goes towards helping someone else that doens't have the same resources to cook at home be able to also cook the same recipes with you. Look at you! Helping others while you are becoming a chef.
